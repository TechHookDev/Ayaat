# Privacy Policy for Ayaat

**Last updated:** February 11, 2026

**Ayaat** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and discloses information about you.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Location Data (Coarse and Fine Location)
Our app requires access to your device's location to calculate accurate **Prayer Times** and **Qibla direction** (if applicable) specific to your geographical position.
*   **Usage:** This data is processed locally on your device to determine prayer times for your area.
*   **Storage:** We do not store your location history on our servers. Your current location coordinates may be temporarily stored locally on your device to avoid repeated requests.
*   **Sharing:** We do not share your location data with third parties.

### 1.2 Device Information
We may collect information about your mobile device, such as the device model, operating system version, and system language, to optimize the app's performance and language settings (Arabic/English/French).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
*   Calculate and display accurate Islamic prayer times.
*   Schedule local notifications for prayer alerts and daily verses.
*   Maintain your preferences (e.g., selected language, notification settings).

## 3. Local Storage
We use local storage (Shared Preferences) on your device to save your settings, such as:
*   Onboarding completion status.
*   Notification preferences (Enabled/Disabled).
*   Selected calculation method for prayer times.

## 4. Notifications
We use local notifications to remind you of prayer times and display daily Quran verses. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not involve sending data to external servers.

## 5. Third-Party Services
This app is built using Flutter and may use third-party libraries that collect standard usage data (e.g., Google Fonts). We do not integrate with third-party ad networks or analytics platforms that track users for advertising purposes.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
